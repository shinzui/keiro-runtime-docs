---
title: "Cross-service integration"
description: "How the two services of keiro-runtime-jitsurei actually talk: message flow, contracts, outbox/inbox, and trace continuity."
---

The two services of `keiro-runtime-jitsurei` share no Haskell code. Everything they coordinate
travels as a versioned message over Kafka: a private decision in one service becomes an **outbox**
row, a worker publishes it to a topic, the other service **consumes** it exactly once into its
**inbox**, and a router or workflow reacts. A single W3C `traceparent` rides along so the whole
journey is one trace.

Start with the message flow for the end-to-end story, then read the mechanism chapters.
