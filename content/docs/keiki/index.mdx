---
title: keiki
description: A pure, dependency-free mathematical core — a symbolic-register transducer.
---

keiki (継起, "successive occurrence") is a pure, dependency-free mathematical core:
a symbolic-register transducer (a kind of state machine) with composable, IO-free
stream and transducer composition governed by algebraic laws. It is "the foundation"
of the keiro runtime in the _pure-semantics_ sense.
